Since opening its first hotel above the iconic windmills of Mykonos Town in 1979, the Myconian Collection has curated the island’s most breathtaking settings. Each property blends contemporary elegance with authentic Cycladic charm, offering refined accommodations, world-class dining, serene wellness experiences, and personalised service. Whether perched above the Aegean, nestled along pristine beaches, or steps from vibrant Mykonos Town, its fourteen luxury hotels - and as many fine destination restaurants and spas - cater to every wish, from romantic escapes to family retreats, or solo adventures.

With fourteen world class hotels in five stunning locations, guests enjoy opulent comfort, exceptional cuisine, rejuvenating spa treatments, and bespoke hospitality, perfect for romance, relaxation, or independent exploration.

  • Platis Gialos
  • Relais & Châteaux

Ambassador

Presiding elegantly over Platis Gialos, the Ambassador offers timeless elegance with a tranquil pool deck, superb fine dining, serene spa facilities that include Thalassotherapy pools, and views that capture the essence of Mykonos.

  • Elia Beach
  • Member of Design Hotels

Avaton

A trend-setting sanctuary with bold aesthetics, infinity pools, and sweeping vistas of the Aegean, Avaton seduces with modern design, superb service and unique celebratory ambiance.

  • Mykonos Town
  • The Set Collection

Deos

Poised above Mykonos town with 180 degree views from its panoramic entertainment deck, Deos is an exquisite sanctuary surrounded by natural beauty – seemingly in a world of its own, yet just a short walk down scenic steps from the lively promenade of the old port.

  • Elia Beach
  • Leading hotels of the World

Imperial

Situated immediately above Elia Beach, with sea views, great restaurants, a superb spa, and a spectacular saltwater pool, the popular Myconian Imperial balances luxury with warm hospitality.

  • Ornos Beach

Kove

A modern retreat just minutes from Ornos beach, Kove has a contemporary take on Cycladic architecture with a constellation of whitewashed rooms, panoramic rooftops, an award-winning spa in cave, and a regular yoga schedule.

  • Mykonos Town
  • Relais & Châteaux

Korali

The elegance of simple lines in both architectural structure and detailing complements the ethos of excellence and savoir-faire that defines Korali’s gracious hospitality, haute cuisine, and exceptional views.

  • Mykonos Town
  • Member of Design Hotels

Kyma

Channelling the iconic 1960s style of laid-back luxury, Kyma’s vibrant pool deck, lush gardens, and art-filled spaces frame rooms which enjoy private pools or Jacuzzis. Mediterranean cuisine and a serene spa complete the unforgettable experience.

  • Ornos Beach
  • Member of Design Hotels

Myconian O

Directly on Ornos beach, the Myconian O embraces outdoor living with luxurious suites opening onto private terraces as a tranquil counterpoint to the vibrant pool deck that is the social heart of the resort.

  • Mykonos Town

Naia

A creative marriage of timeless Mycenaean-inspired architecture and contemporary design, Naia is a boutique gem with intimate suites and scenic areas that exude a refined elegance, complemented by attentive service, an infinity pool, a gourmet Mediterranean restaurant and a scenic bar.

  • Elia Beach
  • Small Luxury Hotels

Panoptis

Perched on the highest point of the rugged hillside, Panoptis Escape is an exclusive retreat with minimalist design and panoramic vistas, embodying its name’s ‘all-seeing’ meaning.

  • Elia Beach
  • Leading hotels of the World

Royal

The Royal Myconian has spacious rooms and suites with private balconies or terraces, superb sea views, a panoramic pool deck, exceptional amenities, a world class spa, and outstanding event facilities.

  • Agrari Beach
  • Relais & Châteaux

Sunrise

Celebrating the luxury of simple pleasures luxury in one of the most idyllic settings on Mykonos, Sunrise sits with its feet in the sands of Agrari, exuding barefoot luxury around a charming pool deck, a taverna under the trees, and an exclusive beach club.

  • Elia Beach
  • Relais & Châteaux

Utopia

Utopia exudes a harmonious blend of contemporary cool, traditional charm and timeless luxury, with impeccable service, superb dining and wellness experiences, and, of course, spectacular sea views.

  • Elia Beach
  • Preferred Hotels & Resorts

Villa Collection

Nestled into the hillside above Elia Bay, the Myconian Villa Collection is a 5-star resort offering a unique luxury experience with spacious suites and elegant private villas, all with uninterrupted Aegean views.

  • All

  • Elia Beach
  • Mykonos Town
  • Ornos Beach
  • Platis Gialos

Our island

Mykonos has retained a wild, natural beauty all its own while evolving into one of the most glamorous 21st-century destinations of choice. Jet-setters, sybarites and sophisticated connoisseurs of the Aegean migrate from all corners of the globe to live out summers of supreme indulgence. With its magnificent sandy bays and clear waters, lively atmosphere and hive of nightlife, the incessant buzz and crush of clubs, restaurants, tavernas, and cafés in Mykonos Town, the island’s seductions are such, that guests return year after year, spellbound by the unique atmosphere and energy.
