What we’re about

The Greater Atlanta Polymer Clay Guild (GAPCG) is a polymer clay guild that meets once a month to have fun creating with the wonderful medium of polymer clay and all other medium that can be combined with it. 
MEETING PLACE:
Chastain Art Center www.ocaatlanta.com
135 Chastain Park Ct., Atlanta, GA 30342

Join our group in sharing and discovering new ways of expressing your creative spirit. First in-person regular meetings are generally free for you to check things out. Come join us, we are always looking for new claymates!
For more details check out our website www.gapcg.org
