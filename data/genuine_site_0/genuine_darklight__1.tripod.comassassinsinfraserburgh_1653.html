This is the story of a group of people living in the wilds of N.E Scotland who have psychopathic tendencies. Rather than break the law, we decided that Steve Jackson's `Killer` would offer an escape from the realities of life.

`Killer` is a live role playing game. The members playing at the time are hired by contract to assassinate another player. What makes life interesting though, is although you have been hired to kill someone, someone else has been hired to kill you. The only person who knows who is killing whom,is the person running the game. No realistic weapons are allowed and all weapons must be "safe".(nothing that could injure anyone is allowed. Members of the public are not considered fair game)

This page is a way for the players to check over time who is the most competent assassin, and who isn't. I intend to try to keep an updated record of the games we have played, scores and money accumulated and spent, and maybe even throw in some pictures of some of the kills as they happen.

I will also try to tell stories of some of the funnier things that happen during the progress of the games.
