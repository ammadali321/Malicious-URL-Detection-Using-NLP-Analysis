Members Only
Stone Bridge Club - 24 Whitebridge Est, Stone, Staffordshire ST15 8LQ

We welcome bridge players. whether beginners, improvers or experienced,  There are numerous sessions which are suited to all levels of play.

Bridge session are on the evenings of Monday and Wednesdays, with an afternoon session on Friday.  See under 'When and Where' for more details.

On evening sessions there is ample parking in front of the club.  Afternoon parking is round the back along the road alongside the railway or on the road side of the carpark.

Although we play upstairs, there is a stair lift.  Toilets are upstairs and downstairs.  On some sessions refreshments are available

Please give us a try.
