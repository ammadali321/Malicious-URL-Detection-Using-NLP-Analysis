      Overview

      Offering massage therapy, a shared lounge, and recreational facilities, the elegant Markazia Suites Beirut hotel lies in the center of the central district of Beirut, just a 14-minute walk from Zaitunay Bay. Featuring 54 rooms with views of the town, the 5-star family aparthotel is 1.4 miles from Horsh Beirut Urban Park.

      Location

      This hotel is situated in the center of Beirut just next door from Qasr Bechara el-Khouri. Maghen Abraham Synagogue is 19 minutes by foot from the Markazia Suites, while vers l'Aeroport bus stop is around 10 minutes' walk away.

      For those traveling from afar, Beirut Rafic Hariri International airport is 10 minutes' drive away.

      Rooms

      The rooms at Markazia Suites hotel include a trouser press and an air conditioner. Some of them comprise a balcony and a dressing room, furnished with a couch and a writing desk. The rooms have a mini fridge bar and tea/coffee making equipment for self-catering along with free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for entertainment. A separate toilet and a sink, along with such amenities as a drier and bath sheets, are also at guests' disposal.

      Eat & Drink

      The accommodation offers its own Jaipur restaurant, where guests can try local dishes. Guests are welcome to the lounge bar to relax with a drink. It also features a lounge area for guests' comfort.

      Leisure & Business

      The Markazia Suites Beirut has a gym.
