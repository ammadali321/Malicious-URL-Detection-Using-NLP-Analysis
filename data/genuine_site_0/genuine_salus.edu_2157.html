Combining two 100+ year legacies under a singular vision of preparing students to make a difference, Salus University, now the Elkins Park Campus, joins Drexel University to lead the future of health care education.

Elkins Park Campus

As part of a comprehensive, global R1-level research university, together we offer professional and graduate degree and certificate programs in health science professions. Drexel Integration

World Class Faculty

Committed to graduating the highest caliber of professionals, our faculty represent some of the best minds in health science and education rehabilitation. Watch Now

Early Clinical Exposure

With a hands-on approach to patient care, an early and sustained clinical experience is what sets our graduates apart from their professional peers. Watch Now

Interdisciplinary Training

Emphasizing quality and continuity with patients and their interprofessional teams, our students are exposed to integrated programs where exploration and collaboration drive holistic healthcare. Watch Now

Prime Location

Experience the best of two worlds. Our vibrant, suburban campus located just outside of Philadelphia offers easy access to unlimited culture, arts, history, sports, and entertainment. Watch Now

Deep Roots, Strong Leaders

Our graduates have a history of leading their professions with progressive thinking for the changing world of health and wellness. Watch Now

Building Community

Our students are part of a community much greater than the University. Close to home and across the globe, we pursue projects that advance the world of health, wellness, and public service. Watch Now
