Chihuahua: Basic Facts & Information

Chihuahua is a populated place LOCATED WITHIN the Abram CDP. In turn, the Abram CDP is located within Hidalgo County.

Chihuahua is located at latitude 26.225 and longitude-98.414.

Since Chihuahua is located within the Abram CDP, all of the census and demographic data for residents of Chihuahua are included as part of the information reported for Abram CDP in the "Profile" tab above.

(Note: CDP = Census Designated Place. A CDP is a statistical area delineated locally specifically for the tabulation of Census Bureau data. While CDPs have specified boundaries, these boundaries have no legal significance.)
