Heathen Wolves

Not too long ago, at this very location there was a website called the Heathen Wolves. Run by an odd fellow who calls himself Bretwalda Randwulf and affiliated with a thoroughly bonkers cult called Woden's Folk, it consisted primarily of text plagiarised from various other websites relating to neopaganism and Germanic mythology.

The site suddenly vanished one day (shortly after RationalWiki revealed that most of its content had been plagiarised - draw your own conclusions there). So with the original and frankly rubbish Heathen Wolves gone, it is now time for the new and improved Heathen Wolves to hit the Internet!
