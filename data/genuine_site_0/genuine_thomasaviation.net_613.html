Welcome to Thomas Aviation Services

Thomas Aviation Services provides commercial aircraft remarketing, acquisition and fleet management expertise to both the financial marketplace and regional aircraft operators worldwide.  With over thirty five years of experience in the the sale, lease and asset management of commuter category aircraft and access to significant portfolios of commuter aircraft, we are ideally positioned to assist with your fleet needs.

Please review our Client List and Transaction List pages to better understand the depth of our aircraft sales and leasing experience. We specialize in the 19 to 50 seat turboprop and the 50 to 70 seat regional jet marketplace with an emphasis on the Beech 1900, Saab 340, Embraer EMB-120, deHavilland DHC-8, Aerospatiale ATR-42, Saab 2000 through theÂ  Bombardier CRJ-100/200 and the Embraer ERJ-145 and E170/190 regional jets. Our clients include passenger and freight operators worldwide.

We also supply fleet management solutions and lease management services such as customer and contract management, asset health monitoring and aircraft return audit and negotiation.

 

Current Projects

Thomas Aviation Services is currently offering Saab 340B and Beech 1900C passenger configured aircraft on behalf of Pacific Coastal Airlines, Embraer ERJ-145 aircraft for ALS Limited of Kenya and have acquisition mandates to acquire two Beech 1900D aircraft on behalf of separate airlines.
