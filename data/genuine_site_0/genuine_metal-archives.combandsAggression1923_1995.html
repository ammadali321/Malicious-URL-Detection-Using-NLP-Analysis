Aggression

Country of origin:
Canada
Location:
Montréal, Quebec (early); Vancouver, British Columbia (later)
Status:
Active
Formed in:
1985
Genre:
Thrash Metal
Themes:
Horror, Death, Violence, Satanism
Current label:
Massacre Records
Years active:
1984-1985 (as Asylum), 1985-1989, ? (as Aggression A.D.), 2005-2006, 2014-present
Contact: [email protected]

Founded by Denis Barthe and Bernard Caudron.
Also recorded a demo in 1985 that was a re-recording of three of the tracks from the first demo, but was only used for promotional purposes and was not made available to the public.
Went by the name Aggression A.D. for a short while (see cover of the Great White North Records edition of the 2004 album).
