About the Montreal Wanderers

The Club

The Montreal Wanderers Rugby Football Club is a bilingual amateur men’s and women’s sports team founded in 1957, well-ingrained into the history, development, and legacy of both Québec Rugby and Canada Rugby.

The Douglas

Our name matches our disposition – the Montreal Wanderers welcome all seasoned and inexperienced rugby enthusiasts from anywhere in the world to come out and have a run with us at our home fields in Verdun!

Our Squads

With new blood and new partnerships, we expect to continue growing our rugby programs and spreading awareness of the beautiful game. If you’re interested in playing rugby, we are always actively looking for players of all levels includes first-time rugby players!

Senior Men

We are an inclusive rugby club that has produced several provincial Québec rugby and national  Canada rugby athletes in both the 7s and 15s games. We field two men’s squads in competitions throughout the year (and are working to build a third), so there’s definitely a spot for everyone, both on the pitch and at the bar!

Coach: Craig BEEMER
Captain: Eliott GOUSSANOU

Inquire now

Senior Women

Since reforming as an independent team in 2018, the “Wander Women” composed of a brilliant mix of talented CEGEP, University, and Senior athletes, have won promotion to the Provincial 1 League and reached the Québec rugby finals under the guidance of seasoned Montreal Wanderer Kaneb Uphill.

Coach: Niamh DEVANEY
Captain: Mathilde THERRIEN-QUINTAL

Inquire now

Juniors (Boys & Girls)

In 2019, our Montreal Wanderers boys’ U19 team won the Provincial 7s Tournament in its first year of inception, and we are committed to building on this success in 2020 and beyond – including developing a parallel girls’ program. As such, we are always on lookout for high-potential juniors to join and grow with our youth program.

Coaches: Kevin THIRUCHELVAM (U19) and Karl CERNOVITCH (Minis)

Inquire now

LaSalle Minis Program (U8-14)

The Montreal Wanderers are committed to growing the sport of rugby across Montréal and Québec. We believe that starting player development at a young age is the best way to produce top-quality players, capable of playing for their province, their country, or perhaps even professionally.

This year, the Montreal Wanderers are very proud to announce our partnership with the City of LaSalle to promote our new Minis program for U8-U14 youths, which will run throughout the season. The Minis’ training sessions are aimed at developing young players that will excel both on and off the pitch.

STARTING SOON! 4-week non-contact tag (flag) rugby program: 

  • Dates: June 03, 10, 17, and 24
  • U8 & U10: 5:30-6:30pm
  • U12 & U14: 6:30-7-30pm
  • Where: Riverside Park (rue Raymond)
  • Cost: $40 for 4 sessions + a t-shirt
 

Any interested parents or players are invited to call or email us to learn more.
