Sackville Nova Scotia is located approximately 33km from Halifax Nova Scotia,Canada. Population = 35,000+(2006)

About Sackville:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lower_Sack ... ova_Scotia

About 106.9 FM (From the web site)

Sanctioned and authorized by Industry Canada and owned and sponsored by Owen Davis and Sons and Daughter Trucking, we will bring coverage of special events in Sackville for the month of May. The station can be heard in stereo on the FM band at 106.9mhz.

It has been a while, seems like forever, but once again due to the concerted efforts of dedicated classic country music fans, radio personalities and community based individuals; we all can enjoy the music that introduced, popularized and cemented the genre of Country Music.

Sackville Radio will provide music for your listening pleasure in a classic country format. This format includes classic country favorites, fantastic fiddle tunes, the best bluegrass, as well as authentic Newfoundland music to tip the iceburg.

Familiar voices will deliver the music to your ears. Frank Lowe, Al Hollingsworth and Joyce Seamone will be some of the regularly featured personalities. Listen for special guests volunteering their time to bring the music to the airways.

Where would today’s country music singers be without the country pioneers who opened the doors, walked the floors, cried the tears, were married (but not to each other), signed the papers, and got the D-I-V-O-R-C-E?
