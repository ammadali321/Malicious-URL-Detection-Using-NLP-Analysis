Interstate 435

Interstate 435 is the outer loop around the Kansas City Metro. It goes through both the State of Kansas and the State of Missouri. In the state of Missouri it is about 53 miles long. Interstate 435 uses the same milage in Kansas and Missouri.
While in the State of Missouri, I-435 is multi-plexed.

I-435 is multi-plexed with...
...Interstate 29, from near Platte City to Kansas City.
...U.S. Rt. 71, from near Platte City to Kansas City.

...U.S. Rt. 50, at Kansas City.
