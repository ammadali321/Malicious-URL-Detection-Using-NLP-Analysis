Story

Thanks for taking the time to visit my JustGiving page.


On 24th July 2010 a small intrepid group of adventurers will undertake the Lyke Wake Walk to raise money for charity.  I have decided that I shall be one of the lucky few.  It is an approximately 40 mile walk across the Yorkshire moors which we aim to complete in under 24 hours. The reason I say approximate is that we are doing this overnight and if our navigation goes wrong it could be a lot further.

I will be raising funds to help a new school that I am becoming involved with in Hammersmith.  I strongly feel that getting good education in our inner cities is key to the future of the next generation. The Hammersmith Academy is being built as you read this and will open it's doors in September 2011.  The funds we are raising will help add value to school and ensure that the students who attend will have a truly excellent education.  I hope that the Hammersmith Academy will make a real difference in the futures of hundreds of young people.  


Thank you for reading this and I hope that you will be generous in your support of the Me and the Hammersmith Academy!
