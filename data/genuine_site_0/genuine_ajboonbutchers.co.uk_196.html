SERVING THE COMMUNITIES OF CHESHIRE FOR OVER 40 YEARS

We stock a wide range of locally sourced produce including beef, lamb, pork, chicken and other poultry, sausages, pies, game such as venison, pheasant and grouse and a huge selection of cheese. As well as offering meat and dairy produce of the highest standard we also provide expert advice on preparing and cooking our meat. All of our in-house butchers and staff are knowledgeable about our produce. We also have a reputation for making some of the finest pies in the North of England!

OUR DELICIOUS RANGE INCLUDES

BEEF

We are proud that all beef sold by A.J Boon is British, with the majority supplied by Mr J Brereton of Nantwich, Cheshire.

LAMB

All our lamb is sourced from local farms.

PORK

All our pigs are supplied locally by E&T Jackson of Tabley, Knutsford and Mr Malcolm Beatie of Allostock and Russel Watson of Frodsham.

POULTRY

We supply a wide range of poultry including whole birds, fillers, breasts and oven-ready dishes.

PIES

All A.J Boon pies are made and cooked fresh on the premises using only locally sourced produce.

GAME

We stock a wide range of in-season game including pheasant, mallard, partridge and venison.

CHEESE & PT

We stock a huge range of locally sourced cheeses from traditional favourites to the most exotic cheeses imaginable.
