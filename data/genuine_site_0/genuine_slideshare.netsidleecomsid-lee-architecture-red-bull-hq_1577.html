SID LEE ARCHITECTURE | Red Bull HQ

9 likes5,607 views
Sid Lee

The document details the architectural design and project description of the Red Bull headquarters in Amsterdam, highlighting its urban location in an old shipbuilding factory that reflects Red Bull's culture in extreme sports and the arts. The design features a duality of spaces and functionality, merging public and private areas while emphasizing natural light and playful elements to encourage creativity and adaptability. The construction reflects modern design sensibilities infused with the brand's ethos, showcasing a responsive and multifaceted workspace.
