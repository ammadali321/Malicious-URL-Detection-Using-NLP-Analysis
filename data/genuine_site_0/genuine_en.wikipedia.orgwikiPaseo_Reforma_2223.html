Paseo Reforma (Reform Mall) is a regional 425,174 sq ft (39,500.0 m2) indoor mall located in Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, Mexico south in the city's retail district. The shopping mall was built from 2007-2008 and was opened in May 2008 at a cost to developer Latin American Realty of $90 million. It was the first mall of its kind in Nuevo Laredo. The mall has three major anchors (Walmart Super Center, The Home Depot, and Cinépolis) and has sub-anchors such as Famsa. The mall is situated in a lot with a total area of 1,356,468 ft² (126,020 m²) which includes 2,000 parking spaces and 6 pads for restaurants outside the mall.[1][2][3][4]
