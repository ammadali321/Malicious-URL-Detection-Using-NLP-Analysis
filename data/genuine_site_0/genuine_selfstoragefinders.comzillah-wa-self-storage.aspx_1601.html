Storage Unit Size Estimates

Identifying the size of a storage unit to rent is an important decision. Having a unit that is too small will prevent you from comfortably storing your belongings safely in the unit, or worse, everything might not fit. Use our storage size calculator to determine which size fits your needs best.
5' x 5'
Size: 25 square feet, equivalent to a small closet
What fits: Small closet or small bedroom, dresser, office supplies, small pieces of furniture, clohing, small number of file boxes
5' x 10'
Size: 50 square feet, equivalent to a large walking closet
What fits: Couch, chair, dresser, small business inventory, seasonal yard equipment
10' x 10'
Size: 100 square feet, equivalent to a small bedroom or half the size of a one car garage
What fits: Furniture and boxes from a one bedroom apartment or home with refrigerator or washer and dryer
10' x 20'
Size: 200 square feet, equivalent to the size of a one car garage
What fits: Furniture and boxes from a two bedroom apartment or home with appliances, or a car/truck and additional small items
10' x 30'
Size: 300 square feet, equivalent to the size of a very large room (largest size at most facilities)
What fits: Furniture and boxes from a fully furnished 3-4 bedroom home with appliances, patio furniture, bikes and seasonal items
