    About This Property

    We offer Self-Guided Tours by Appointment Only. Discover relaxed, modern living amid a lush landscape with sweeping Bay views at The Landing at Jack London Square in Oakland, CA. Each of our apartment homes for rent is designed in modern style and features a combination of wood-style flooring and carpet, quartz countertops, stainless steel appliances, and your own private patio or balcony. Additional conveniences include spacious storage closets and an in-home washer and dryer and select homes also include a fireplace. With our offering of studio, one-, and two-bedroom floor plans, we're sure to have the right one for you. Around our pet-friendly community, enjoy amenities such as our community courtyard and barbecue area, perfect for hosting friends and neighbors. When it's time to venture beyond the neighborhood, getting around is simple. BART is less than a mile from our community, and the nearest AMTRAK station is right outside. Catch the San Francisco Bay Ferry at nearby Oakland Jack London Square Terminal, located less than a mile from your front door. Major interstates 880 and 980 are just a few blocks away making commuting easier than ever. Price reflected is base rent and does not include mandatory or optional fees. Detailed fee information and fee schedules can be found on the community's Leasing Info page on our Essex website.
