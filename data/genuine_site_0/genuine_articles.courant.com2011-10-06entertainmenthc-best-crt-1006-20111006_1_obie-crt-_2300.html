    David Patrick Kelly Stars in ‘Our Town’

    Share this:

    • Click to share on Facebook (Opens in new window) Facebook
    • Click to share on Bluesky (Opens in new window) Bluesky
    By
    UPDATED: September 24, 2021 at 12:03 AM EDT

    David Patrick Kelly has played some formidable and strange characters over the years. Now the Obie-winning actor will portrays the Stage Manager in Connecticut Repertory Theatre’s production of “Our Town.” The Thornton Wilder play opens CRT’s season Oct. 6 to 16 at the Nafe Katter Theater, 802 Bolton Road, at the University of Connecticut in Storrs. Robert Ross Parker (Obie-winning “Vampire Cowboys”) directs.

    UConn Alumna Mary Cadorette also joins the cast as Mrs. Gibbs. Cadorette is best known for her television show with John Ritter, “Three’s a Crowd,” her performance in Broadway’s “42nd Street” and her reign as Miss Connecticut 1975.

    The cast will also feature Equity actors David Sitler as Dr. Gibbs, Brad Bellamy as Mr. Webb, Carolyn Popp as Mrs. Webb and Ken O’Brien as Joe Stoddard/Constable Warren/Professor Willard.

    Tickets are $17 to $29. Student discounts available. Information: 860-486-4226 and crt.uconn.edu.

    Frank Rizzo

    (frizzo@courant.com)
