Our Mission

Here at Wyatt Performance Horses, we are committed to high quality service.  Whether it's a horse in training, breeding, lessons, helping you buy or sell a horse, we put our clients first.

Our primary focus is to further the conformational excellence of the quarter and paint horse, train phenomenal horses, and help others on their way to greatness. The people that make this happen are Timothy Clawson, and Jamey and Jessie Wyatt. Its a family business. Tim has over 50 years experience as a horse trainer, shoer, breeder and showman.  His daughter Jamey Wyatt followed in his footsteps and built a ranch with her husband Jessie Wyatt in the hills of Pala, CA. You can find Jamey researching lines and breeding schedules constantly trying to perfect the quarter horse breed. Whether you're an owner looking for a champion rider to train and show your horse, or its your first time riding, our goal is to bring out the greatness in every horse and rider.

  Please feel free to contact us with any questions, or stop by and see us in Pala, CA.
