WELCOME TO THIS WEB SITE DEDICATED TO THE MEMORY OF JACLYN LINETSKY AND VADIM SCHNEIDER











By a terrible day, an icy morning, Jaclyn Linetsky and Vadim Schneider, two rising stars and wonderful teenagers, lost their young life in a car crash on the road to the set of "15/love". They were only 17. They were just light, love, joy and talent. Two wonderful stars on Earth, now angels in Heaven, with a constant big smile and big heart. But sadly, the life lets them no chance to live their dreams and their perfect happiness.

This is my tribute for them, to show how much they could be missed and loved even after many years.





In loving memory of

Jaclyn Michelle Linetsky
A star on hearth, A star in heaven
January 8, 1986 - September 8, 2003

Vadim Schneider
Once an artist, Always an artist
March 10, 1986 - September 8, 2003
