DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC HEALTH AND SOCIAL SERVICES (DPHSS)

Dipattamenton Salut Pupbleko Yan Setbision Susiat

OUR MISSION

To assist the people of Guam in achieving and maintaining their highest levels of independence and self-sufficiency in health and social welfare.

 

OUR VISION

Empowered people of healthy mind and body living in a safe environment throughout their lifetime.

 

OUR VALUES

Dedicated to eliminating health disparities for all, customer-service oriented, professional and efficient government agency, and responsive to health and social needs in the community.
