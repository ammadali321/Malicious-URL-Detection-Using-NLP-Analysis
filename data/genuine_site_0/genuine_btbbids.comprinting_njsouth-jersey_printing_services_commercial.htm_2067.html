Printing Services Business Network Enters Southern New Jersey Printers Arena

Printing services companies in Southern New Jersey, many of whom are long-time BTBbids.com clients are joining the new BTB Printing Services Business Network.

New Jersey based BTBbids.com, industry leader in the government bid reporting industry, has spent the last 6 months working on the new BTBbids.com Business Network. Commercial printing companies and custom printers throughout the east coast, especially old business associates of BTB are benefiting from these efforts

Already covering the dumpster rentals and cleaning services industries, BTBbids.com asked its clients what industry to work on next... the answer was "Printing Services (commercial and custom)!" The new network is designed to connect companies who sell products and services to quickly and easily connect with the end-users who need them.

Kyle Yoder, BTBbids.com co-CEO, extends an open invitation for companies and business minds across the country to partner up on this (the Printing Services Business Network) and other projects in order to find new ways to achieve the greatest success possible in the new year.
