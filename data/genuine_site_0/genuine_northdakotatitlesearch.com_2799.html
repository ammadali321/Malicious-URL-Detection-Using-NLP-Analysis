Welcome! We are N.D. Property Title -- the industry's leading provider of residential and commercial property research in the state of North Dakota. We perform due diligence on any property within the state, no matter the county. Through the past few decades, we have evolved in many ways, but continue to deliver on our promise to give our clients any kind of title search they need; all the while maintaining competitive prices and guaranteed accuracy. Our standard title reports include Chain of Title, Current Owner, and Environmental Lien searches, and typically include any and all open and pertinent records. Thanks to extensive relationships throughout the state, we have top turn-around times in the title search industry. Our staff are on-hand to pull relevant recorded documents from county repositories to include in your report.

By staying up to date with new technology, N.D. Property Title is able to handle high volumes of orders. We've made our system scalable and software robust. No time is lost because the system is working even when our in-house staff is not. Automation conveniently pulls helpful property data to supplement existing property info, such as address, parcel number, owner name, etc... That way, by the time our title examiners see your order, they can use the additional property data to complete and verify your report. We use artificial intelligence to improve overall quality, provide consistency checks, and catch any errors.

Our objective, as always, is to provide you with what you need to fulfill title search requirements, so we will happily customize any of our research solutions to suit your needs. Our knowledgeable staff is available to answer questions about title work or any of the services we provide. Make sure you're getting the right product by giving us a call at 1-701-401-8335. A quick list of services we provide are Title & Deed Chains, Two Owner, Current Owner Search, Judgments and Liens, Doc Image Retrieval, and custom encumbrance reports.
