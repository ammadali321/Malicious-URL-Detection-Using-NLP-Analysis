Three assistant professors join School of Social Work

The University of Washington School of Social Work is excited to announce that Kamryn Morris, Melanie Morris and Lauren White will join the School as assistant professors this fall quarter. Their research interests span prevention science, oral health equity and mental health in Indigenous communities. We are proud to welcome these outstanding scholars to our faculty and social work community.

Sarah Porter wins Grand Challenges for Social Work Doctoral Award

Sarah Fatemeh Porter, Ph.D. candidate at the UW School of Social Work, was selected as one of 14 recipients of the prestigious 2025 Grand Challenges for Social Work Doctoral Award. Founded in 2012 by the American Academy of Social Work and Social Welfare, the Grand Challenges for Social Work is a groundbreaking initiative to identify and collectively take on our nations toughest social problems.

Rona Levy elected to Washington State Academy of Sciences

Professor and Associate Dean for Research Rona Levy has been elected to the Washington State Academy of Sciences. WSAS members are nationally recognized for their scientific and technical expertise and Dr. Levy is among 36 scientists and educators across the state, 12 of which are University of Washington faculty, announced on July 17 as new members.
