about

Senior Dog Appreciation Society is a band made up of Sacramento veteran musicians and dog lovers. Until now, mainstay bassist Michael Palmer (Mumbo Gumbo) and drummer Neil Franklin (Kai Kln) have been playing in separate musical circles. Now their pairing makes up a fantastic rhythm section to play with.

This music is blues, soul and loud R&B grooves.

“as comfortable and warmhearted as any faithful old canine pet…Overall, there’s a consistent, softly-glowing analog vibe.”
-S Victor Aaron, Something Else Reviews

“..the trio’s all-instrumental debut leans into a gritty brand of soul, blues and sturdy R&B trodding the backroad routes between Muscle Shoals and Stax Records.”
-Aaron Davis, Sacramento Bee
