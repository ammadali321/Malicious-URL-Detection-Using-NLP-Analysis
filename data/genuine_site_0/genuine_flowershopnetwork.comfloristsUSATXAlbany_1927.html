    Make Your Flower Delivery Go Smoothly!

  1. Make sure that you have all the data the florist will need--recipient name, business name, address, phone number, and any special times for delivery.

  2. Know what you want your card message to say--most flower shops have someone experienced enough to help you write something perfect to go with your flowers, but having an idea before you call will save you lots of time.

  3. If you plan to send flowers to a hospital, have the name and address ready. If you know of any restrictions for flower delivery (in case the recipient is in a restricted area, for example), let the florist know.

  4. When you send flowers to a funeral home, have the name, address ready. If there are certain viewing times that will affect the flower delivery, let the florist know.

  5. When working with a florist near Albany and you need flowers delivered within the city limits, make sure that you ask the florist if they can make it to Albany on the day you need to send flowers and that the florist goes to your Albany postal codes.
