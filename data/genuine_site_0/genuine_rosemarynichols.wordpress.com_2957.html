Trees! Beautiful Trees!

June 22, 2011 //
0
Trees are Earth’s endless effort to speak to the listening heaven” ≈ Rabindranath Tagore

This slideshow requires JavaScript.

My First Tree

I remember a few years ago when people would be around trees and say things like, “Can you feel them?” or “Wow so much energy coming from that tree.”  I remember looking at them at the time and thinking they were crazy.   And then I had my first tree “experience”.  I remember coming around the corner and see this tree and just crying.  Me the grounded one weeping because of a tree.  But there was no controlling the flood.  It just came out.

Ever since then I understand…

There is an essence that I feel now anytime I am around a tree.  Some more than others but all of them speak to you in a way that if you are listening you can hear it or should I say Feel it!  Enjoy them….

When there’s snow on the ground, I like to pretend I’m walking on clouds ≈ Takayuki Ikkaku

I love the Winter time.  There is a quiet piece that exist only in the winter.  I love to go outside during a snow and listen… You can actually hear the snow flakes falling when you listen closely.  There is also something about winter that gives my permission to be a kid again. I allows to bundle up, go outside and just be goofy.  I love those snow that allows you to go outside and throw snowballs, make snow angels or just go for long peaceful walks. 

And then there is nothing like sitting by the fireplace looking out the window and just watching the world go by.  Ahhh such a peace.
