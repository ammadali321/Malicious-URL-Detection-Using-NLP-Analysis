    Gino Vannelli

    Gino Vannelli (born , 1952) is an Italian-Canadian singer, songwriter, musician and composer. Born in Montreal, Quebec, Vannelli is one of three sons born to Russ and Delia Vannelli. Russ, his father, was a big band musician.
